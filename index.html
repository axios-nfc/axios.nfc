Uses of NFC

NFC (Near Field Communication) allows smartphones to communicate with a tag using a simple tap. Axios NFC tags make everyday digital interactions fast and contactless.

NFC tags can be used for digital payments, sharing visiting cards, leaving Google reviews, opening restaurant menus, connecting to WiFi, sharing social media links, and accessing websites instantly.

Businesses such as restaurants, shops, hotels, offices and professionals can use NFC tags to improve customer experience and make interactions faster.
